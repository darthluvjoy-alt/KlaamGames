Privacy Policy for Eclipse Relics

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Eclipse Relics (the "Game") is developed and operated by Klaam Games ("we", "us", "our"). Klaam Games is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. This policy explains what data the Game uses, why it is used, who receives it, and the choices and rights available to you.

Optional analytics is off until you choose. The Game asks for permission in the main menu before optional analytics, attribution, or marketing measurement starts. Declining does not limit gameplay. You can change the choice at any time under Settings → Analytics data.

How to delete your account and data

In the Game, open Settings → Delete Account and confirm the request. The Game sends the request to our server and removes local data only after the server confirms that the PlayFab player account has been permanently deleted.

If you cannot open the Game, email darthluvjoy@hotmail.com. Include your in-game display name and, if available, your player ID so we can identify the correct account. Do not send passwords or payment-card details.

Deletion removes the player account, saved progress, inventory, currencies, leaderboard and event entries, guild membership and applications, player-linked chat and activity entries, and other player-linked backend data. If other members remain, a guild itself, including its name and settings, may remain and leadership may be reassigned. Information that must be retained for legal, transaction, fraud-prevention, or security purposes may be kept only for the required period. Requests sent to optional analytics providers are queued after the PlayFab deletion is confirmed and are normally completed within 30 days.

Optional analytics and your choice

If you accept optional analytics on iOS, the Game may send analytics data to PlayFab analytics. Tenjin and Meta attribution are disabled in the iOS soft-launch build, the Game does not access IDFA, and it does not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

If you accept optional analytics on a supported Android build, the Game may send analytics and attribution data to PlayFab analytics and Tenjin. Tenjin may send relevant attribution, device, interaction, and purchase-event data to advertising partners such as Meta to measure our campaigns. Eclipse Relics does not display third-party ads, and we do not sell personal data.

You can withdraw consent at any time under Settings → Analytics data. New optional analytics collection stops immediately and queued analytics events are cleared. Withdrawal does not affect processing that took place before withdrawal. Core PlayFab data needed for sign-in, saved progress, multiplayer, purchases, security, and account deletion continues to be processed so the Game can work.

On Android, you may also reset or limit the advertising ID under your device's privacy and ads settings. The in-game analytics choice controls whether Eclipse Relics connects to its optional attribution service.

Service providers and recipients

  • Microsoft Azure PlayFab: guest authentication, cloud saves, game services, CloudScript, multiplayer data, leaderboards, and optional analytics after consent.
  • Apple App Store and StoreKit: payment processing, purchase confirmation, and store receipt handling on iOS. Apple handles payment credentials under its own privacy terms.
  • Google Play Billing: payment processing and purchase confirmation on Android. Google handles payment credentials under its own privacy terms.
  • Tenjin: optional install attribution, analytics, and marketing measurement on supported Android builds after consent. It is disabled in the iOS soft-launch build.
  • Meta: may receive optional Android campaign-measurement events through Tenjin after consent. It does not receive events from the iOS soft-launch build.

Providers process data under their agreements and privacy terms. Some providers may process data outside your country, including in the United States, using the transfer safeguards available under applicable data-protection law.

Legal bases

Where the European Economic Area or United Kingdom data-protection rules apply, we process core account, gameplay, multiplayer, and purchase data because it is necessary to provide the Game you request. We process security and fraud-prevention data based on our legitimate interests in protecting players and the service, and we retain information where required by law. Optional analytics, attribution, and marketing measurement are based on your consent.

Retention

Core game data is retained while the account remains active and until it is deleted. Chat, reports, and guild activity may be kept in limited rolling histories or for as long as reasonably needed for the feature, moderation, security, or support. Purchase, fraud-prevention, and legal records may be retained for the period required by applicable law or a legitimate security need. Optional providers apply their own documented retention periods; an account-deletion request also queues the identifiers needed for provider-side deletion.

Security

Data sent between the Game and its online services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Access is limited to systems and people that need it for operation, support, security, or legal compliance. No online system can guarantee absolute security.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, receive a portable copy, and withdraw consent. You may also complain to your local data-protection authority. To exercise a right, contact us using the address below. We may need enough information to verify that the request concerns your account.

Children

Eclipse Relics is intended for players aged 18 and over. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the Game, providers, or legal requirements change. The current version and revision date will remain available on this page. Material changes will be communicated where required.
