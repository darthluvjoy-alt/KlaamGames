Privacy Policy for Eclipse Relics

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Eclipse Relics (the “Game”) is developed and operated by Klaam Games (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you play the Game, how we use it, and the choices you have. By playing Eclipse Relics you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

The Game does not require you to create an account with your name, email address, or phone number. We collect the following:

  • Account identifier: an anonymous account ID is automatically generated for your device so we can save your game progress. It is not linked to your real-world identity.
  • Display name: a nickname you choose inside the Game. It is visible to other players in chat and on leaderboards.
  • Purchase history: a record of in-app purchases you make. Payments are processed by Google Play Billing; we never receive or store your payment card details.
  • In-app messages: messages you send in the Game's chat features.
  • Device identifiers and technical data: a device identifier together with analytics, app-interaction, and diagnostic data used to operate and improve the Game.
  • Advertising ID: your device's advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS) and App Set ID. We use these only to measure how our own marketing campaigns perform — not to show you ads. See “Advertising ID and marketing” below.

How we use your information

  • To run the Game and save your progress across sessions.
  • To provide chat, guilds, leaderboards, and other multiplayer features.
  • To process and restore in-app purchases.
  • To analyse performance, fix crashes, and improve the Game.
  • To measure how our own marketing campaigns perform (see “Advertising ID and marketing”).

How your information is stored and shared

Game data is stored on our backend service provider, Microsoft Azure PlayFab, which processes data on our behalf. In-app purchases are handled by Google Play Billing. We also work with Tenjin, a marketing-analytics provider that receives your advertising ID, device data, and purchase events so we can measure how our own marketing campaigns perform; Tenjin may pass attribution data to advertising networks such as Meta for that measurement. We do not sell your personal information, we do not use your data to show you third-party ads, and the Game does not display any ads.

Advertising ID and marketing

Eclipse Relics contains no advertising and shows no ads. We use your device advertising identifier solely through our attribution provider Tenjin to understand which of our own marketing campaigns brought new players to the Game. We do not use it to build advertising profiles about you.

You can reset or limit your advertising ID at any time: on Android via Settings → Privacy → Ads; on iOS via the App Tracking Transparency prompt and Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Data security

Data transmitted between the Game and our servers is encrypted in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS.

Children

Eclipse Relics is intended for players aged 18 and over. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your game data for as long as your account remains active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated progress at any time from inside the Game via Settings → Delete Account, which removes your data from our servers and your device. You may also request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us at the email address below. Because accounts are tied to your device, please include enough information (such as your in-game display name) for us to identify your account. We will delete your data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
